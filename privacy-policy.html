Privacy Policy

Margino · Effective: June 1, 2025 · Last updated: June 1, 2025
Margino ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Margino mobile application — a Reseller Fee Calculator. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1 Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to provide and improve the app. The categories are:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics on how features are used (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash Reports: Automatic crash logs sent via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs. These do not include personally identifiable information.
  • Account Data (if you sign in): Email address and profile data stored securely in Supabase.
  • Purchase Data: Subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details — these are handled by the App Store / Google Play.
  • Support Messages: If you contact support, your message and email are stored to respond to you.
ℹ️ We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use collected data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Providing core calculator functionality and syncing your history.
  • Managing your Pro subscription and unlocking premium features.
  • Diagnosing crashes and improving app stability.
  • Sending transactional emails (e.g., support replies) via Resend.
  • Displaying ads to free-tier users via Google AdMob (rewarded and banner ads).

3 Third-Party Services

Margino integrates with the following third-party providers, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google) — Analytics, Crashlytics, Push Notifications. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Advertising for free users. Privacy Policy
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery. Privacy Policy

4 Local Data Storage

Some data is stored locally on your device:

  • Calculation history (limited to last 100 entries) stored using shared_preferences.
  • Sensitive tokens (auth session) stored using flutter_secure_storage, which uses the device Keychain (iOS) or Keystore (Android).

This local data is never transmitted to our servers unless you are signed in and have sync enabled.

5 Advertising & Rewarded Ads

Free-tier users see ads powered by Google AdMob. Rewarded video ads allow free users to export data as CSV. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads in accordance with Google's privacy policies. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

6 Push Notifications

We may send push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for app updates or important announcements. You can disable notifications at any time from your device's notification settings.

7 Children's Privacy

Margino is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

8 Data Retention & Deletion

We retain account data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Local device data can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

9 Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All API communication is encrypted via HTTPS / SSL (Nginx + Let's Encrypt).
  • API keys are compiled into native code using envied to prevent extraction.
  • Our backend enforces rate limiting (100 requests per 15 minutes per IP) and HTTP security headers via Helmet.js.
  • Sensitive environment variables are never exposed publicly.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11 Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or data requests, please reach out:
